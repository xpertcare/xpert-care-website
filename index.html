Services

Residential Habilitation

We offer personalized residential habilitation services that are tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. Our goal is to promote independence, enhance life skills, and empower our clients to live more independently.

We design tailored programs that promote independence and enhance quality of life. We offer in-home support, assisting clients in living on their own while providing the necessary guidance and care.

Skill Development Programs

We design programs focused on skill-building and personal growth. Our training programs help individuals develop daily living skills, life skills, and social skills to thrive in their environments.

Behavioral Habilitation

We teach coping strategies, stress management techniques, and healthy lifestyle habits. We aim to improve behavior, communication, interaction, and emotional regulation, promoting stronger relationships and a balanced life.

We design personalized programs focused on improving communication, emotional regulation, and social skills. We teach positive coping strategies and techniques to manage behaviors, empowering individuals to thrive on their own.

Supported Living

Xpert Care offers supported living programs designed to provide in-home support that aligns with individual needs. Our trusted staff ensures that each client can live in their own home with the assistance they require to function independently.

Daily Life Activities

Our skilled staff provide in-home assistance with a variety of daily living activities, including: cooking, cleaning, grocery shopping, and managing money, medications, appointments, and transportation.

Our comprehensive supported living programs are designed to promote autonomy and self-reliance. We provide guidance and support to help clients manage daily tasks, handle finances, coordinate transportation, schedule appointments, and administer medications.

Support

Personalized Plans

At Xpert Care, our personalized care plans and dedicated team provide a holistic approach to residential habilitation. We focus on the following areas to support adults with developmental and intellectual disabilities:

  • Skills Training and Development: We help individuals learn and practice daily living skills, life skills, and social skills to enhance their ability to live independently. Our programs are designed to develop and strengthen abilities, promoting autonomy and self-reliance.
  • Behavioral Management: Our staff teaches effective coping strategies and stress management techniques. We focus on improving behavior, managing emotions, and developing healthy lifestyle habits. This helps individuals interact positively with their environment and build stronger relationships.
  • In-Home Care and Support: Our services include in-home care and supported living programs that are tailored to individual needs. We provide assistance with personal care tasks and daily living activities. Our goal is to help individuals live in their own homes.
  • Independent and Fulfilling Lives: We are dedicated to empowering individuals to lead fulfilling lives. Our personalized support promotes personal growth, independence, and self-reliance. We design programs that enhance life skills, support healthy lifestyles, and foster positive interactions.

Our approach is holistic and client-centered, ensuring that each individual receives the care and support they need to thrive.
